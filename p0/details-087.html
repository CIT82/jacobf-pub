SCP-087 is located on [REDACTED] campus, disguised as a janitorial closet. Access requires specific voltage and key rotation. Inside, it's a dark staircase with 13 steps descending at a 38-degree angle to a semicircular platform. The staircase turns 180 degrees at each platform, limiting visibility to about 1.5 flights. Despite numerous attempts, exploration hasn't brought subjects closer to the presumed source of distress calls, estimated 200 meters below. There's uncertainty about SCP-087's endpoint. Four explorations have encountered SCP-087-1, a face-like entity causing intense paranoia and fear in subjects. It's unclear if these reactions are abnormal. Post-exploration, knocking sounds were reported, leading to padding being installed on the door, stopping the noises.
