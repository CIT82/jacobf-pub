Canons

One extremely important thing to know about the SCP universe is that there is no true canon. With thousands of authors contributing for well over a decade, it would be almost impossible to keep everything straight, so instead of even trying, the community dove further into multiverse theory than I’ve seen anywhere else. However, sometimes the community will enjoy one person’s reimagining of the world and want to contribute to it. These are some of the more popular ones.
